Materials: 30 counters: 15 of one color and 15 of another color
Players: 2

  • Each player starts with 15 counters of their color. Each counter represents one soldier.
  • At the start of each round, each player secretly chooses how many soldiers to deploy.
  • Once each player has decided, both players will shout, "3, 2, 1, deploy!" and place their deployed soldiers at the center of the play area.
  • The player who deployed more soldiers takes all armies in the center of the play area.
  • These armies are placed in the player's side pile. They cannot be redeployed.
  • If both players deployed the same number of soldiers, each player puts only their own soldiers into their side pile.
  • After that, the next round starts.
  • Once one player runs out of soldiers to deploy, the game ends.
  • Each undeployed soldier at the end of the game kills an enemy soldier. The opponent does not get any points from the dead soldiers.
  • The player who controls more soldiers (side pile + undeployed soldiers) wins the game.
  • In the case of a tie, play again.
